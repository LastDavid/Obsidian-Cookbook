# Deli Snack Box

Servings: 1

## Ingredients
- [ ] 2 ounces thinly sliced turkey breast- [ ] 1 large egg, hard boiled- [ ] 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes- [ ] 1 ounce sharp cheddar cheese, cubed- [ ] 4 pita bites crackers- [ ] 2 tablespoons raw almonds

1) Place turkey, egg, tomatoes, cheese, crackers and almonds into meal prep containers.
