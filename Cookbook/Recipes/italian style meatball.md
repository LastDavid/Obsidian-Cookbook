# Italian Style Meatball

Adapted from http://www.geniuskitchen.com/recipe/italian-style-meatball-recipe-245418

tags: Beef, Pasta

Servings: READY IN: 1hr 15mins SERVES: 6-8

## Ingredients
- [ ] 1 lb ground beef- [ ] 1 ⁄2 cup breadcrumbs- [ ] 1 ⁄2 cup grated parmesan cheese- [ ] 1 ⁄3 cup chopped onion- [ ] 1 ⁄4 cup milk- [ ] 2 eggs- [ ] 2 garlic cloves, minced- [ ] 1 ⁄2 teaspoon pepper- [ ] 1 ⁄2 bunch flat leaf parsley, chopped- [ ] 4 tablespoons olive oil- [ ] 32 ounces spaghetti sauce

## Steps
1) In a large bowl, beat the eggs.

2) Add the rest of the ingredients, blend with hands until combined.

3) Shape into 1 inch balls.

4) Brown in a skillet with just enough of the olive oil so that meat balls do not stick to the bottom of the pan.

5) In 5-quart Dutch oven, over medium high heat, bring spaghetti sauce to a simmer.

6) Add meatballs. Bring to boil. Partially cover and simmer 25 minutes or until meatballs are thoroughly cooked.
